Redesigning Centauro shopping experience

The sports and fitness gear market is booming in Brazil, in 2016 it has grown 22% reaching a record of 1,9% of Brazilian's GDP. Centauro, a long-standing leader in the segment with hundreds of stores in the country, sees its market threaten by big online business with aggressive promotions strategy. Centauro’s differential is their strong presence in physical stores and broad range of products. How might we show Centauro's prospective customers the advantages of buying online from a business that is multichannel, offering multiple flexible shipping?
