My Design Process

Discover

Empathizing with the users and understanding their needs, pain points and emotions they feel in their journey

Define

Defining what exactly are the problems that are the most important for our users

Diverge

Discovering inspirations and ideating possible solutions for the problem

Decide

Deciding what are the most promising solutions we want to test and refine it

Prototype

Making the solution feel real for users to test it

Test

Testing and learning with real users for iterating our way to the final solution
